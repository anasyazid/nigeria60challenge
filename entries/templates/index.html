The Challenge

On the 1st October 2020, Nigeria will be celebrating its diamond jubilee as it turns 60 years old.

To celebrate Nigeria’s Diamond Jubilee (60th Anniversary), The Federal Government of Nigeria under the Leadership of President Muhammadu Buhari, GCFR has approved an inclusive National Independence Celebration program that will see the thematic and creative aspects of the event are designed by Nigerians for Nigeria. The FGN wants to use this opportunity to harness the power of Nigeria’s creative minds to create a new brand identity around the anniversary theme, that will be celebrated in the public space for one year. The new brand will include a slogan and graphic merchandise. FGN is looking for innovative and creative individuals or timelines with great critical problem solving and graphic design skills to produce the new brand identity for Nigerians to mark its 60th Anniversary.

Timeline

With the Announcement of the theme of Celebration by His Excellency, President Muhammadu Buhari GCFR on the 1st September, 2020. The president will also mark the opening of the portal to receive submissions and close on the 10/09/2020. Applications will undergo voting process by Nigerians, verification screening and the appointed juries will review shortlisted applications. The best three (3) will make a presentation to the President and Winners will be announced on the 1st October 2020.

  • August 2020 Lauching
  • September - 10 - 2020 Applications Close
  • September - 13 - 2020 Public Voting Close
  • September - 20 - 2020 Finalists Selected
  • October - 1 - 2020 Presentation Day
